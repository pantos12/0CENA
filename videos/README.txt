BACKGROUND VIDEO INSTRUCTIONS

Place your background video file in this directory with the name:
background.mp4

Video Requirements:
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec recommended)
- Resolution: 1080p (1920x1080) recommended
- Duration: 15-30 seconds (it will loop)
- Content: Nature scenes, park footage, or other relevant visuals
- File size: Ideally less than 10MB for fast loading

For the best user experience, choose a video that:
- Has smooth, subtle motion
- Doesn't have rapid scene changes
- Has good contrast with text overlay
- Contains muted, natural colors

The video will automatically play in the background with:
- Autoplay enabled
- Sound muted
- Looping enabled
- Responsive scaling

If no video is found, the page will display normally but without the video background effect.
